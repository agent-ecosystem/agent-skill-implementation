# Contributors

Thank you to everyone who has contributed platform findings, proposed checks,
improved benchmark skills, or otherwise helped this project.

## How credit works

Contributors are listed here by name (or handle) and what they contributed. If
you submit a PR, we'll ask whether and how you'd like to be credited. You can
choose to be listed by your real name, GitHub handle, or not at all.
